HR Policy Update (Effective Jan 2024)

1. Remote Work: The company has shifted to a fully remote model; employees can work remotely 5 days a week.
2. Expense Reporting: Travel expenses must be submitted within 15 days of the trip to be eligible for reimbursement.
3. Equipment: Home office stipends are no longer provided, but employees can expense up to $500 for a monitor.
4. Core Hours: We maintain core working hours of 10 AM to 3 PM EST to facilitate collaboration.
5. Vacation: Employees now receive 20 days of PTO per year.
