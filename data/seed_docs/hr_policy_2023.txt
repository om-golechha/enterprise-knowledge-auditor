HR Policy (Effective Jan 2023)

1. Remote Work: Employees are permitted to work remotely up to 2 days a week.
2. Expense Reporting: All travel expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the trip.
3. Equipment: The company provides a standard $500 stipend for home office equipment.
4. Core Hours: Core working hours are 10 AM to 3 PM EST.
5. Vacation: Employees receive 15 days of PTO per year.
